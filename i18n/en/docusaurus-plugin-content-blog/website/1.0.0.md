## 1.0.0: Official Release

This release bumps the site version to `1.0.0` because the basic shape is now in place: homepage, docs, blog, multilingual content, comments, and article surfaces are all working steadily. There will still be changes, of course, but this feels ready to be treated as an official version.

It is not a full stop that says everything is finished. It is more like a solid beginning. Earlier versions were mostly about building the frame, tuning the style, and improving the experience. With 1.0.0, I want the site to move into a more natural rhythm of use.

## The Blog Will Be More Active

The blog section will become more active from now on. It will not be limited to technical posts either.

There may be casual complaints, small moments from daily life, loose thoughts that do not need a whole framework, or notes I simply want to leave somewhere. Technical writing will still be here, but I do not want this place to feel like a cold knowledge base forever.

I would rather it become somewhere I can open casually: sometimes serious, sometimes relaxed; sometimes for organizing experience, sometimes just for recording a mood from that moment.

## What's Next

After 1.0.0, the site will keep evolving little by little. Docs will be filled in, blog posts will appear, and the page experience will keep getting tuned.

So, the official version is out. This place should get a bit more lively from here.
